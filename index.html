Zynar began his musical journey as a dnb and hip hop dj during his college years. Life and career goals made pursuing music difficult. However once a raver, always a raver. Six years ago Zynar decided to give music another shot or else live with regret. Sampling from hip hop and inspired by sound design from dnb, Zynar offers a fresh take on house music.
